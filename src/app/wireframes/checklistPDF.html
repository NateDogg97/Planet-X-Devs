5 Critical Website Issues Costing You Customers

A quick audit checklist from someone who fixes these daily

Hi there! I'm a web developer who reviews dozens of business websites each month. These are the 5 most common (and costly) issues I find.

Check off what applies to your site. If you can't check at least 3 boxes, we should talk.

1

Your Site Takes Forever to Load

CRITICAL IMPACT
  • Homepage loads in under 3 seconds
  • Mobile loading feels fast
  • Images don't take forever to appear
Business Impact: Amazon found that every 1-second delay costs them $1.6 billion in sales annually. For small businesses, I've seen 3-second load times cut conversions in half.
Quick Fix: 90% of speed issues come from unoptimized images. Compress them and you'll see immediate improvement.
2

Google Says Your Site is "Not Secure"

CRITICAL IMPACT
  • Padlock icon shows in browser bar
  • No security warnings appear
  • URL starts with https:// (not http://)
Business Impact: 85% of online shoppers avoid unsecured websites. Plus, Google penalizes non-secure sites in search rankings.
Quick Fix: Most hosting providers offer free SSL certificates. It's usually a one-click install in your hosting control panel.
3

Mobile Visitors Can't Use Your Site

CRITICAL IMPACT
  • Site looks good on smartphones
  • Buttons are big enough to tap
  • Text is readable without zooming
  • Menu works on mobile devices
Business Impact: 67% of users are more likely to buy from a mobile-friendly site. With 60%+ of traffic from mobile, this is make-or-break.
Quick Fix: Test your site on your phone right now. If you're pinching to zoom or can't tap buttons, your customers are struggling too.
4

Nobody Can Find You on Google

HIGH IMPACT
  • Each page has a unique title
  • Your business shows up when searched
  • Page descriptions make sense
Business Impact: 93% of online experiences begin with a search engine. If you're not showing up, you're invisible to potential customers.
Quick Fix: Start with unique page titles and descriptions. It's basic SEO that many sites completely miss.
5

Visitors Don't Know What to Do Next

HIGH IMPACT
  • Clear "Contact Us" or "Get Started" buttons
  • Phone number is clickable on mobile
  • Forms are simple and actually work
  • Each page has an obvious next step
Business Impact: You can have thousands of visitors, but without clear calls-to-action, they leave without converting. It's like having a store with no checkout.
Quick Fix: Look at each page and ask "What do I want visitors to do here?" Then make that action impossible to miss.
